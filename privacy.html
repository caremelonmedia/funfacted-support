Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

TL;DR - Your Privacy in Simple Terms:

FunFacted stores everything locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or share any of your personal information. Your data never leaves your phone.

Introduction

FunFacted ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the FunFacted mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

FunFacted collects minimal information to provide you with daily facts. All information is stored locally on your device:

  • Notification Preferences: Whether you want to receive daily notifications
  • Notification Time: Your preferred time to receive daily facts
  • App Usage Data: Which facts you've viewed (stored locally only)
  • Settings: Your app preferences and configurations

How We Use Your Information

We use the locally stored information to:

  • Send you daily fact notifications at your chosen time
  • Track which facts you've already seen
  • Remember your app settings and preferences
  • Provide a personalized experience

Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not:

  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Store your information in the cloud
  • Have access to your personal data
  • Share your information with third parties

Your data remains under your complete control and is protected by your device's security features.

Third-Party Services

FunFacted does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No tracking or behavioral profiling

Permissions We Request

FunFacted requests the following permissions:

Notifications

Purpose: To send you daily facts at your chosen time

Required: Optional - you can use the app without enabling notifications

How to revoke: Settings → FunFacted → Notifications

Children's Privacy

FunFacted is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. Since all data is stored locally on the device and nothing is transmitted, children's privacy is fully protected.

Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored on your device and accessible through the app
  • Deletion: Delete the app to remove all data permanently
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications at any time in Settings
  • Export: Your data is not stored externally, so there's nothing to export

Data Retention

Data is stored on your device for as long as you have the app installed. When you delete FunFacted from your device, all associated data is permanently removed.

International Users

Since FunFacted stores all data locally on your device and does not transmit information internationally, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns. Your data stays in your country, on your device.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

FunFacted does not collect, sell, or share personal information. California residents have the same privacy protections as all users: complete data privacy with local-only storage.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):

  • Legal basis: Legitimate interest in providing app functionality
  • Data controller: You are the data controller of your own information
  • Data processing: All processing happens locally on your device
  • Rights: Full control over your data at all times

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect:

  • Changes in legal requirements
  • Improvements to the app
  • User feedback

When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Post the new Privacy Policy in the app and on this website
  • Notify you through the app if changes are significant

Your continued use of FunFacted after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Payment Information

FunFacted is a paid app. All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or billing address.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

Email: support@funfacted.app

We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 48 hours.

Commitment to Privacy

At FunFacted, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. We've designed our app from the ground up to protect your privacy by:

  • Storing everything locally on your device
  • Never collecting personal information
  • Not using analytics or tracking
  • Giving you complete control over your data

We will never compromise these principles.
